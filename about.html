About Ashok Power Solution

Ashok Power Solution stands as a beacon of excellence in the electrical and automation industry across Haryana, India. Founded with a vision to revolutionize the way electrical services are delivered, we have consistently pushed the boundaries of innovation while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide reliable, efficient, and cutting-edge electrical solutions that empower businesses and communities to thrive. Today, we are proud to be recognized as one of the leading electrical and automation service providers in the region.

We specialize in providing top-notch electrical and automation services to industries and households across Haryana, India. Our expertise lies in delivering innovative solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Specialized Services

AC/DC & Servo Drives

Advanced AC and DC drive systems with servo motor controls for precise speed and position control in industrial applications. Our solutions ensure optimal performance and energy efficiency.

PLC, HMI, UPS, Compressors

Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC), Human Machine Interface (HMI) systems, Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) solutions, and industrial compressor installations and maintenance.

LT, HT, PCC, MCC, APFC Panels

Low Tension (LT) and High Tension (HT) panels, Power Control Centers (PCC), Motor Control Centers (MCC), and Automatic Power Factor Correction (APFC) panels for comprehensive power management.

Sensors, Modules, Weight Machines

Industrial sensors, control modules, and weighing machine installations. We provide complete automation solutions with precision measurement and control systems.

Meet Our Founder - In Detail

Mr. Ashok Kumar

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 25 years of pioneering experience in the electrical and automation industry, Mr. Ashok Kumar founded Ashok Power Solution with a vision to transform the electrical services landscape in Haryana and beyond.

A graduate in Electrical Engineering from the prestigious Delhi College of Engineering, Mr. Kumar began his career with leading multinational corporations before venturing into entrepreneurship. His deep understanding of both traditional electrical systems and modern automation technologies has been instrumental in establishing the company's reputation for innovation and excellence.

Under his leadership, Ashok Power Solution has grown from a small local service provider to a recognized industry leader, serving clients across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. His commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction continues to drive the company's success and expansion.

"Our goal is not just to meet our clients' electrical needs, but to exceed their expectations through innovative solutions and exceptional service," says Mr. Kumar.
